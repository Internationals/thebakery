The International Bakery

International Bakery has been cherished for its classic Indian, Brazillian, Italian baked goods, vintage decor and warm, inviting atmosphere.
We have thoughtfully expanded the bakery from its first West Village shop to locations worldwide.
Today, we can be found in New York City, Los Angeles, Chicago, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Beirut, Kuwait City, India and Doha.
In addition to our current location in Louisville.
International Bakery is franchising internationally and will soon open in Tokyo, Japan; Moscow, Russia; and Mexico City, Mexico.
Patrons are also invited to shop, where we offer a selection of freshly baked cupcakes, cookies, brownies and bars, as well as corporate gift packages and seasonal assortments.
